Project Seva is a non-profit for people looking to make real change. It aims to connect donations directly to people most in need after natural disasters. As a donar, you can choose exactly what you are paying for and where it is going and you are assured your donation will be used and make a real impact.

"Each of us as human beings has a responsibility to reach out to help our brothers and sisters affected by disasters. One day it may be us or our loved ones needing someone to reach out and help."

Michael W. Hawkins, American Red Cross
